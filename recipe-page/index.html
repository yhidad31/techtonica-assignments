Food Wishes' Recipe for Minestrone Soup

To be honest, I've never used this recipe, but I do enjoy minestrone soup; it's an easy way to get your veggies in for the day.

Image Credit: TheGirlOnBloor

Ingredients:

  • 3 oz pancetta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 4 minced garlic cloves
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 (28-oz) can plum tomatoes, crushed fine
  • 2 cups water, plus more as needed
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried Italian herb blend (mine was thyme, oregano, rosemary, basil) red pepper flakes to taste
  • 1 cup freshly shucked cranberry beans (aka shelling beans)
  • 2 or 3 cups chopped cabbage
  • 1 (15-oz) can garbanzo beans, drained
  • 1 bunch swiss chard, chopped
  • 2/3 cup raw ditalini pasta
  • extra virgin olive oil, Parmigiano-Reggiano, and fresh Italian parsley to garnish the top

Recipe Instructions (It's an easy soup, so don't stress too much about it)

  1. Brown the pancetta in olive oil
  2. Take out the pancetta
  3. Brown the onions in the pancetta grease
  4. Add celery, garlic cloves and seasonings
  5. Add all legumes
  6. Add tomatoes, water and broth
  7. Let simmer for a reasonable amount of time
  8. Add ditalini
  9. Add greens when pasta is cooked through
  10. Serve in cute bowls
